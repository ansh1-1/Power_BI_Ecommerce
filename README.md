# Power_BI_Ecommerce
Developed an interactive Power BI dashboard to analyze revenue, profitability, customer behavior, and product performance. Leveraged KPI tracking, trend analysis, and dynamic filters to identify profitable segments, monitor business performance, and support data-driven decision-making.
The **E-Commerce Sales Performance Dashboard** is an interactive Business Intelligence solution developed using **Power BI** to analyze revenue, profitability, customer behavior, product performance, and purchasing patterns. The dashboard provides stakeholders with a comprehensive view of business performance through KPI tracking, trend analysis, and dynamic quarter-wise and category-based filtering. It tracks key metrics including **Total Revenue (438K), Gross Profit (37K), Total Quantity Sold (5,615 units), and Profit Margin (8%)**, enabling effective performance monitoring. The dashboard incorporates visualizations such as monthly profit trends to identify peak and loss-making periods, state-wise revenue analysis to highlight high-performing markets, category-wise quantity analysis to evaluate customer demand, top customer analysis to identify high-value customers, payment mode distribution to understand customer payment preferences, and profit by sub-category analysis to uncover high-margin products. By integrating these insights into a single interactive platform, the dashboard answers critical business questions related to revenue generation, profitability, sales volume, regional performance, customer value, product demand, payment behavior, and yearly profit trends, thereby supporting data-driven decision-making, strategic planning, and business growth.
